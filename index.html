About Institut Tecnològic de Barcelona (ITB)

ITB is a high-performance educational center operating at the forefront of technology and sustainability. We train future IT professionals while promoting an eco-conscious mindset.

Our facilities function as a "living laboratory": from 24/7 server labs to smart solar panel integration, the center not only teaches technology but actively uses it to improve efficiency.
